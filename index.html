Research Interests

I have broad interests in theoretical and algorithmic machine learning. My previous research mainly concentrates on multi-view learning, in which I proposed new models (KDD’18, TKDE) and new framework (AISTATS’19). I am also interested in finding clever provable exact/approximate optimization techniques for computationally difficult problems.
